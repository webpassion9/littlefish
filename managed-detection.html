World-class Managed and
Endpoint Detection and Response

With over 100 years of combined experience in cyber defence, the Littlefish team offers world-class Managed Detection and Response (MDR) and Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) services which deliver valuable insight and monitoring into the networks and systems of your business, including the analysis of your core infrastructure, as well as the applications used, the user behaviour and the endpoints.

As part of our MDR and EDR service packages, we also provide advanced threat intelligence, combined with proactive threat hunting and 24/7 security monitoring to ensure a rapid and efficient incident response to high-end cyber threats. With detailed incident analysis and strategic advice from our experts, we leverage big data to give you full visibility and constantly evolve your endpoints and networks.

How our mdr and edr services benefit your organisation
24/7 cyber threat monitoring

We monitor all your on-premises and cloud systems 24/7, 365 days a year to ensure your assets and data are protected at all times.

Rapid incident response

We are constantly on alert to provide a rapid incident response to cyber threats, contain the attack and deploy remediation and recovery procedures.

Enterprise level detection

Intelligent EDR Technology

We use the best technology on the market to speed up the threat detection and response time. We use market-leading tools to organise, evaluate and prioritise all cybersecurity events across all endpoints.

Enhanced cloud security

Ensuring the safe collaboration via multiple endpoints across various locations is crucial. Our EDR services are designed to keep your team collaborating and sharing information without data the threat of breaches.

Working in partnership

Our managed detection and response services are tailored to your needs – we can work to support your in-house team or take full responsibility for your cyber security.

How does Littlefish MDR help your team?

Our managed detection and response services lift the pressure off your in-house team’s shoulders. We take on the responsibility of overseeing and protecting your network, systems and endpoints around the clock, so your staff can focus on what they do best without having to worry about cyber threats. Whether you want us to fully manage your security needs or you would like us to support your IT specialists, the Littleish MDR team is flexible and we can adapt to your specific requirements.

Here’s what we can do for your business as part of our managed detection and response services:

  • We investigate potential threats and gather threat intelligence, using machine learning and AI technologies combined with the expertise of our leading cyber team.

  • We analyse, classify and evaluate security events to identify patterns allowing us to improve your cyber security.

  • We monitor for threats 24/7 and launch investigations into any suspicious activity flagged as a potential security issue to ensure nothing slips through the cracks.

  • We offer a fast incident response to contain any threats, deploy an efficient remediation strategy and help your business recover as quickly as possible.

Why is endpoint threat detection important?

Maintaining visibility across all endpoints at an enterprise level can be challenging. Failure to detect an attack quickly enough subsequently slows down the response times and increases the chance of serious damage being inflicted on your business by malicious actors.

Cyber attacks are evolving rapidly with large corporations and even government organisations being targeted using identity theft to gain access and ransomware to keep company data hostage. Using proactive endpoint detection tactics allow us to have superior situational awareness and pinpoint malicious activity that would otherwise have gone unnoticed.

Who needs endpoint detection and response?

As we mentioned before, enterprises working from multiple locations, as well as governments, are often exposed to more Cyber threats than smaller businesses, as the potential payoff from a successful Cyber attack is much higher for the malicious actor.

At Littlefish, we work with a number of large corporations that require complex endpoint detection and response services. Whatever industry you are in, whether it’s retail, communications or financial services, if your company is struggling to keep up with the numerous Cybersecurity incidents, our team can assist you by strengthening your security at an enterprise level.

Steve Robinson
Chief Executive Officer

Hailing from a background in Operations, Littlefish CEO Steve Robinson is responsible for strategy and organisational development, as well as being heavily involved in meeting prospective customers and helping to shape the design of their service solutions.

Since acquiring Littlefish in 2010, Steve has overseen the business’s significant growth, marrying a laser-sharp focus on world-class service quality, with the creation of a profitable and sustainable business model. Steve has worked in the IT Industry since leaving school (sadly for Steve more than 20 years ago now), and prior to Littlefish, held senior roles in Computerland UK Plc and Capita Plc, where he was responsible for running a variety of services as well as helping to secure significant new business wins.

Outside of work Steve’s main preoccupation is with his young children, whose interests and hobbies have become his own – something he’s sure many parents will sympathise with! Being fortunate enough to live only a short walk from the City Ground, when Steve get’s the chance, he likes to follow his favourite football team, Nottingham Forest.
