Wanted: Giant Slayers.

By delivering exceptional managed IT Services across the public and private sectors we’re frequently winning business from the multi-£bn global IT outsourcing giants. If you’ve always fancied yourself as a giant slayer then we’d love to talk to you.

Join the growing team

Littlefish is a recognised and fast growing credible alternative to the ‘usual suspects’ in the IT Outsourcing (ITO) and Managed IT Service market. And our reputation for providing industry-leading services as well as exceptional IT Careers is growing fast too. In the past few years, we have more than quadrupled in size, dramatically increasing both our revenues and workforce, and we’ve got no intention of letting up. With an impressive customer base, a strong pipeline of new business, and a proven track-record of growth, we are interested in hearing from, and finding, the best talent, to join our award-winning business and to be part of our continued success as we continue to create new IT jobs.

Our employees are our biggest asset and our biggest focus. Career progression for everyone is important to us. It’s crucial that our staff can flourish within Littlefish, so we encourage collaborative personal development, and have an ‘internal first’ policy when it comes to promotion for new roles. So, if you invest your time and effort in us, then we’ll invest in you too. Hopefully some of our current vacancies are suitable for your skills and experience. If not then don’t worry, our recruitment requirements are ever-changing, so just check back with us again in a few weeks. Or, if you prefer, send us a copy of your CV with a covering letter explaining why you would like to join Littlefish and what you would bring to our organisation, and we’ll let you know when suitable opportunities arise.

Steve Robinson
Chief Executive Officer

Hailing from a background in Operations, Littlefish CEO Steve Robinson is responsible for strategy and organisational development, as well as being heavily involved in meeting prospective customers and helping to shape the design of their service solutions.

Since acquiring Littlefish in 2010, Steve has overseen the business’s significant growth, marrying a laser-sharp focus on world-class service quality, with the creation of a profitable and sustainable business model. Steve has worked in the IT Industry since leaving school (sadly for Steve more than 20 years ago now), and prior to Littlefish, held senior roles in Computerland UK Plc and Capita Plc, where he was responsible for running a variety of services as well as helping to secure significant new business wins.

Outside of work Steve’s main preoccupation is with his young children, whose interests and hobbies have become his own – something he’s sure many parents will sympathise with! Being fortunate enough to live only a short walk from the City Ground, when Steve get’s the chance, he likes to follow his favourite football team, Nottingham Forest. He is also a keen(ish) runner and is currently training for the 2020 London Marathon. For the last few spare minutes of the day Steve likes to read, especially on subjects like current affairs, psychology, philosophy, politics and economics – which he often finds to be a good aid to a sound night’s sleep!
