Our employees are our biggest asset and our biggest focus

The Littlefish vibe is the result of our #peoplematter approach to employment and employees, and is testament to the organisational culture we promote. Our approach is simple, we put our people first, every time and without hesitation.

Some of our benefits
City Centre Location

Located in the heart of Nottingham’s Lace Market and the bustling West Street, Sheffield, our offices are right in the heart of the city.

Holiday Allowance

Great work deserves great play, with generous holiday allowances and long service rewards.

Promote From Within Policy

We have an internal first policy when it comes to promotion for new roles and every appropriate new position is advertised in-house.

Gamification

Compete against other Service Desk pods and engineers, earn Fish Coins that can be traded for amazing real-world rewards and compete for monthly prizes.

Break Out Areas

Our break out areas are fully equipped with the latest in gaming and our very own table tennis and pool table.

Littlefish Academy

Get access to the industry leading online training platform and gain globally recognised certifications and accreditations through our Academy.

Unlock a potential £4,500.00 of increased earnings outside of annual pay reviews as you progress, with 2 exam attempts in work time.

Relaxed Dress Policy

We want you to be comfortable as you deliver exceptional service to our customers so we have a relaxed dress policy.

LinkedIn Learning

Unlimited access to over 10,000 expert-led courses to help you discover and develop new skills.

£800 Refer a Friend

Earn up to £800 through our refer a friend employment scheme

Long Service Rewarded

We recognise our loyal employees’ commitment with Long Service Awards beginning at 5 years.

Regular Company Socials

Get to know your colleagues and let your hair down to celebrate a job well done with our regular company-wide socials.

Starfish Reward Scheme

Whenever you support our customers, they have the chance to nominate you for our Starfish reward scheme.

Colleagues with the most nominations each month are rewarded with prizes including Amazon vouchers. Great service is rewarding.

Summer Family BBQ

Summer BBQ with food, drink and entertainment for the kids. All employees and families invited.

Christmas Party

Enjoy a night out with your colleagues and celebrate the year’s achievements in style, on us.

Pizza Friday

Great work deserves great pizza. Enjoy fresh pizza on us on the last working Friday of each month.

Eat Fresh Fruit

Enjoy free fruit for a healthier treat. Officially counts as one (or more) of your five-a-day.

Have a Cup on Us

Recharge whenever you like with free refreshments.

Employee Wellbeing

Now more than ever we know that your health is important to you and we feel the same.

Employees of Littlefish benefit from Simply Health personal health plan which gives you money back on dental costs, new glasses or contact lenses, chiropractor or physio appointments, access to online GP services, counselling services, Gym membership discounts and much more!

Steve Robinson
Chief Executive Officer

Hailing from a background in Operations, Littlefish CEO Steve Robinson is responsible for strategy and organisational development, as well as being heavily involved in meeting prospective customers and helping to shape the design of their service solutions.

Since acquiring Littlefish in 2010, Steve has overseen the business’s significant growth, marrying a laser-sharp focus on world-class service quality, with the creation of a profitable and sustainable business model. Steve has worked in the IT Industry since leaving school (sadly for Steve more than 20 years ago now), and prior to Littlefish, held senior roles in Computerland UK Plc and Capita Plc, where he was responsible for running a variety of services as well as helping to secure significant new business wins.

Outside of work Steve’s main preoccupation is with his young children, whose interests and hobbies have become his own – something he’s sure many parents will sympathise with! Being fortunate enough to live only a short walk from the City Ground, when Steve get’s the chance, he likes to follow his favourite football team, Nottingham Forest. He is also a keen(ish) runner and is currently training for the 2020 London Marathon. For the last few spare minutes of the day Steve likes to read, especially on subjects like current affairs, psychology, philosophy, politics and economics – which he often finds to be a good aid to a sound night’s sleep!
