Our Pod is here for you 24/7

The Littlefish IT service desk is the beating heart of our managed IT service provision, offering organisations a window to the world of their users and acting as a key interface for Business Relationship Management. Providing global 24/7/365 availability, across multiple languages and time zones – we offer an always-on service, ready to support users whenever, and wherever, they are working. With user-experience at its core, the Littlefish Service Desk features an industry leading average response time of just 10 seconds.

Opting for outsourced IT services is a cost-effective investment. We help customers across a variety of industries achieve the level of support and user experience they desire through tailored IT Service desk services, delivered by 2nd and 3rd line technical engineers. Whilst our managed Service Desk is staffed by seasoned experts who can quickly resolve technical issues, they also genuinely care about your business and your users. Their priority is to understand your users’ activities from both a technical and business perspective so our IT Service Desk becomes a natural extension of your organisation.

Our innovative ‘Pod’ system allows us to deliver users a tailored and personalised service, engendering familiarity, mutual trust and co-operation. Coupled with our shift-left approach to continual enhancement of business knowledge this allows us to achieve First Contact Resolution rate of 70% or greater.

Outsourcing your IT services let’s you get on with running your business

Our outsourced IT help desk services are designed to consider and deliver tangible outputs in terms of improved business performance for businesses of any size. Providing enterprise-level IT support, unlimited scalability and affordable pricing structure, the team works around the clock to fulfil the needs of your organisation, no matter how large it may be.

The IT experience of your users can be enhanced to promote more agility and improved quality output. Furthermore (but often overlooked), highly available, better performing IT services, with expert support on hand when it’s needed to get your users working again, can engender employee health and well-being. As a consequence of these deliverables, workforce productivity levels are increased. And it’s likely that our highly-attuned service model will also enable you to effect cost-efficiency gains. What’s not to like!

  • Choose what to outsource: We can take care of everything for you or we can only manage a certain level of support, be it 1st or 3rd line. Just let us know what you need.
  • IT Help Desk tailored to your company: Contact us around your core working hours, we even offer Out of Hours support for businesses that run 24/7.
  • Proactive Monitoring: We offer 24/7 IT systems monitoring to prevent issues before they happen. It’s the best way to not only reduce but eliminate downtime altogether.
  • Transparent IT Support Desk Reporting: Keep track of every support ticket raised and see how it progresses until it’s resolved. You can also access transparent reports about the overall performance of our IT support desk.
  • Compliance & Licensing: Stay compliant with the latest industry regulatory standards and never miss on a license renewal again!
  • Certified Service Provider:Our outsourced IT help desk services comply with the (SDI) and ITIL best practice and we have international ISO accreditation.

Steve Robinson
Chief Executive Officer

Hailing from a background in Operations, Littlefish CEO Steve Robinson is responsible for strategy and organisational development, as well as being heavily involved in meeting prospective customers and helping to shape the design of their service solutions.

Since acquiring Littlefish in 2010, Steve has overseen the business’s significant growth, marrying a laser-sharp focus on world-class service quality, with the creation of a profitable and sustainable business model. Steve has worked in the IT Industry since leaving school (sadly for Steve more than 20 years ago now), and prior to Littlefish, held senior roles in Computerland UK Plc and Capita Plc, where he was responsible for running a variety of services as well as helping to secure significant new business wins.

Outside of work Steve’s main preoccupation is with his young children, whose interests and hobbies have become his own – something he’s sure many parents will sympathise with! Being fortunate enough to live only a short walk from the City Ground, when Steve get’s the chance, he likes to follow his favourite football team, Nottingham Forest. He is also a keen(ish) runner and is currently training for the 2020 London Marathon. For the last few spare minutes of the day Steve likes to read, especially on subjects like current affairs, psychology, philosophy, politics and economics – which he often finds to be a good aid to a sound night’s sleep!
